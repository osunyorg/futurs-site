FUTUREs is a modular and cooperative game for citizens to become agents of change. It offers an optimistic vision based on imagination and action for players to support climate, biodiversity, social justice and wellbeing.

The game combines strategy and creativity: it can be used as a board game, educational inventory and workshop support.

FUTUREs' 128 cards will give you a better understanding of today's issues while inspiring you to join the happy adventure of co-creating tomorrow's world.

It can be played by 2 players or more, aged 6+, with 5+ alternative game rules.

The Future Council is an invitation to imagine the world of tomorrow so that it meets the needs of future generations.¬†

With the FUTUREs cards in hand, participants will be following in the footsteps of their great-grandchildren, adopting a forward-thinking approach.

In a spirit of intergenerational justice, the workshop is an opportunity for CEOs, policymakers, and leaders to sketch out the contours of tomorrow's world.

The concept is simple: a group of citizens responds to a problem by proposing a collective vision and an action plan in line with the needs of future generations.

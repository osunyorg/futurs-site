FUTUREs is an educational card game that aims to help players explore, imagine and create desirable and sustainable futures. The game is made up of several sets of cards, each addressing a specific theme such as climate change, inequality, wellbeing and biodiversity. The cards are designed to be easy to understand and use, making them accessible to all individuals, whatever their level of prior knowledge.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

FUTUREs has been developed to meet the needs of students, teachers, and community leaders. The game is based on 4 pillars:

1. More than 5 adaptable game modes: FUTUREs offers more than 5 different game modes, making it easy for anyone to adapt it to their learning objectives. Individuals can use FUTUREs for group activities, class projects or homework.

2. Collaborative and interactive workshops: FUTUREs is designed to encourage collaboration and interaction between citizens. Anyone can use FUTUREs to organise group workshops where people work together to explore different maps and imagine scenarios for a desirable and sustainable future.

3. Active and playful learning: FUTUREs offers an active and playful learning approach, where citizens are encouraged to explore, imagine and create. This can help individuals understand topics better and retain information longer. What's more, FUTUREs' playful approach can help keep people interested and motivated.

4. Inspiring and collaborative content: FUTUREs is designed to help citizens develop their creative thinking and work together to find solutions to climate and societal challenges. Anyone can use FUTUREs to help people develop their collaborative spirit and critical thinking skills.

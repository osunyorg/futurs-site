128 cards to imagine a thriving future


FUTUREs aims to empower citizens to become agents of change through several card games, an interactive card directory, as well as workshops to help citizens connect with their emotions and imagine the future.





A card game
Multiple rules to play inclusive games with easy dynamics to have fun while learning. Game modes include individual, team, competitive and cooperative rules.





An interactive card directory
A directory of the 128 cards to better understand, learn and take action for the future. Articles, podcasts, news articles, reports, art performances, and solutions are featured to help players make sense of the world.





Educational resources
A set of activities to use the cards helps people connect, get creative, and take action. Fit for educators, students, companies, policymakers, and changemakers.





A transformational intiative
FUTURs aims to set a new standard with a fully committed initiative in service to active citizenship.♻️ Committed and ethicalA sustainable product manufactured in Europe and standing for strong ethical values.💡 Inspiring and hopefulA joyful and inspiring approach to current issues focused on solutions.🎲 Dynamic and interactiveA tool facilitating interactions and social cohesion for the development of new skills.🚻 Inclusive and modularA game with simple rules that adapts to all types of audiences and contexts.🌍 Original and empoweringA unique approach to unite, inspire and encourage action for a better world.
